Job 22:28 · Friday

WEEK 3
THE REFINER'S FIRE
ANCHOR VERSE OF THE DAY

“But he knoweth the way that I take: when he hath tried me, I shall come forth as gold.”Job 23:10 (KJV)

REFLECTION

The refiner's fire is not meant to destroy you but to purify you. God turns up the heat not in anger but in love, drawing out the impurities so that His image shines clearer in you. The Refiner watches closely, removing the fire the moment He sees His reflection. Trust His hand in the heat today, for you will come forth as gold, more precious than before.

I DECREE

1

God refines me as gold and I come forth purified.

Job 23:10, Mal 3:3
2

My trials produce patience and perfect character in me.

James 1:3-4
3

The trial of my faith is more precious than gold.

1 Peter 1:7
4

When He has tried me, I shall come forth as gold.

Job 23:10
5

He sits as a refiner and purifier, and He purifies me.

Malachi 3:3

PROPHECY

You will come forth as gold! The fire that tries you is purifying, not destroying you!

PRAYER

Thank You Father for refining me. (Mal 3:3)

Let me come forth as gold. (Job 23:10)

Perfect Your work in me through every trial in Jesus' name. (James 1:4)

A WORD IN VERSE

The Refiner's fire is love and not His wrath,He melts to draw the dross and shame away;And when He sees His face along my path,He lifts me, gold, into the perfect day.
