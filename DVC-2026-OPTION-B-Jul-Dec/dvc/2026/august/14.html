Job 22:28 · Friday

WEEK 2
GOODNESS OF GOD
ANCHOR VERSE OF THE DAY

“Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life: and I will dwell in the house of the LORD for ever.”Psalm 23:6 (KJV)

REFLECTION

The goodness of God is not occasional but relentless, following you all the days of your life. It is the kindness that leads to repentance and the mercy that meets you in unexpected places. When you train your eyes to expect His goodness, you begin to see it everywhere. Look today for the good hand of God, and you will surely find it.

I DECREE

1

The goodness of God overtakes me in ways I never saw coming, and it leaves me amazed.

Psalm 23:6
2

I see the goodness of the Lord in the land of the living, not merely in the hereafter.

Psalm 27:13
3

Pleasant surprises follow me, for God has stored up great goodness for those who fear Him.

Psalm 31:19
4

I taste and see that the Lord is good, and my testimony makes others hunger for Him.

Psalm 34:8
5

His goodness leads me to repentance and crowns my year with abundance. Glory!

Psalm 65:11

PROPHECY

His goodness is overtaking you in ways you did not see coming! Pleasant surprises are starting to follow you everywhere!

PRAYER

Thank You Father for Your unfailing goodness. (Ps 107:1)

Let me taste and see that You are good. (Ps 34:8)

Surround me with Your goodness in Jesus' name. (Ps 31:19)

A WORD IN VERSE
