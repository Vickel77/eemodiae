DAILY VICTORY CONFESSION 2026 - OPTION A (Year > Month, scrolling)
====================================================================

STRUCTURE
  dvc/index.html          Landing page (choose year: 2026)
  dvc/2026.html           Choose a month (July - December)
  dvc/2026/<month>.html   Full month as one scrolling page

All six months are live: July, August, September, October,
November, December (184 days total).

HOW TO USE
  Open dvc/index.html in any browser. Click 2026, then a month.
  Each month is one long scrolling page. As you scroll, the top
  bar (Days / Share / Next) stays fixed and updates per day.

FEATURES
  - Each day: Theme, Anchor Verse (KJV), Reflection, 5 Decrees,
    Prophecy, Prayer, and a closing quatrain (A Word in Verse).
  - Each month has its own colour.
  - Share button on every day (uses phone share sheet; copies link
    on desktop).
  - @thehjcw and www.eemodiae.org on every day.

DEPLOY TO eemodiae.org
  Option 1: Drop the dvc/ folder into your site's public/ folder
            and redeploy.
  Option 2: Drag the dvc/ folder onto Netlify Drop or Cloudflare
            Pages, then link to it from your site.
